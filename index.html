M.Starc the Best Bowler in the World

Date of Birth: 30th January 1990
Nationality: Australia
Profession: Cricketer

Mitchell Aaron Starc is an Australian international
cricketer who plays for the Australian national team and New South Wales in domestic
cricket. A left-arm fast bowler and a lower order left-handed batsman, he is Australia's fourth
highest wicket-taker in both Test and One Day International (ODI) cricket.
